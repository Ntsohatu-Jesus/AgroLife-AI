Maize farming in Cameroon is best done during the early rainy season.

Plant maize between March and April in most regions. Use well-drained fertile soil.

Spacing should be 75cm between rows and 25cm between plants.

Apply NPK fertilizer two weeks after planting.

Common diseases include maize streak virus and leaf blight.

To prevent disease:
- Use resistant seeds
- Avoid overcrowding
- Practice crop rotation

Harvest maize after 3 to 4 months when grains are dry.
